# 🎬 Russian Recruiting Spot — "I'm Not Dmitry" (Reveal Hook)
**Bridges & Allies · ~35s · two presenters (Dmitry → Marwan reveal)**

A pattern-interrupt ad: a Slavic-looking presenter ("Dmitry") opens in Russian,
then a hard cut reveals it's really you (Marwan) — and pitches the job. Great for
stopping the scroll of Russian-speaking viewers.

---

## 🎞 Shot 1 — "Dmitry" · Russian · 0–5s
- **On screen:** a Ukrainian/Slavic-looking man, friendly, speaking to camera
  (HeyGen avatar OR stock-style avatar). Casual-professional.
- **RU:** «Привет! Меня зовут Дмитрий, я из Киева, и я ищу новую возможность
  в Дубае…»
- **Gloss:** "Hi! My name is Dmitry, I'm from Kyiv, and I'm looking for a new
  opportunity in Dubai…"
- **Then:** hard cut (record-scratch / freeze-frame).

## 🎞 Shot 2 — The reveal · Marwan · English · 5–12s
- **On screen:** YOU, Marwan (film yourself on a phone — the real face after the
  fake lands the joke. Or a Marwan HeyGen avatar.)
- **EN:** "Okay… honestly? I'm not Dmitry. I'm **Marwan**. But if your name *is*
  Dmitry — or you speak Russian — I'm looking for **you**."

## 🎞 Shot 3 — The pitch · Marwan · English · 12–30s
- **EN:**
  "**Bridges & Allies** is hiring. Juniors, fresh graduates, or seasoned agents —
  if you're ready to build a real career, this is your moment.
  And our commission scheme? One of the most rewarding in Dubai — your effort,
  your reward.
  Here's the truth: the market is correcting right now. That's *exactly* when
  smart people get in — before it heats back up. **Let's light the candles on
  the cake before someone else eats it.**"

## 🎞 Shot 4 — CTA end card · 30–35s
- **EN (VO + on-screen text):** "Position yourself **today**. Join the
  **Bridges & Allies** team."
- **End card:** logo + "DM me 'DUBAI' to join 🇷🇺" + your handle.

---

## 🗣 Optional: keep the reveal bilingual
If you'd rather the reveal also reassure Russian speakers, add a 2-sec Russian
tag after Shot 2:
- **RU:** «Да, я говорю и по-русски тоже — добро пожаловать в команду.»
  *(Yes, I speak Russian too — welcome to the team.)*

## 🎬 How to produce
**Easiest (authentic):**
1. **Shot 1 (Dmitry):** HeyGen → pick a Slavic-looking avatar → Russian voice →
   paste the RU line → generate.
2. **Shots 2–4 (Marwan):** film yourself on your phone reading the English (or
   HeyGen Marwan avatar). The real-face reveal is funnier.
3. **CapCut:** stitch Shot 1 → hard cut → Shots 2–4. Add a record-scratch SFX at
   the cut, captions (RU on Shot 1, EN on the rest), logo, music, end card.
   Export **9:16**.

**All-AI (no filming):** do every shot in HeyGen with two avatars; same edit.

## 💬 Captions
- Shot 1 (burn RU + small EN gloss): «Привет! Меня зовут Дмитрий…» / "Hi, I'm Dmitry…"
- Shots 2–4 (burn EN): the lines above, one short line at a time.

## ⚠️ Keep it honest (so it converts and protects your brand)
- **Commission:** "one of the most rewarding" is fine as a claim — but be ready
  to state the real % when people ask. Don't promise numbers you won't pay.
- **Juniors:** only say "build your career / training" if you actually mentor them.
- No income or visa guarantees.
- The "Dmitry" gag is clearly a joke revealed in 5 seconds — keep the reveal fast
  so it reads as fun, not bait.
