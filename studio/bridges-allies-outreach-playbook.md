# 📣 Bridges & Allies — Outreach Playbook
**Recruiter (Smart AI Team) · find + message the right agents, honestly**

Two audiences, two pitches:
- **Juniors / career-switchers** → join *your* Dubai off-plan team (training given).
- **Established agents abroad** → become *partners* who refer clients to Dubai.

Pair every message with the 60s video. Personalize the first line — never blast.

## 🔁 Follow-up sequence (stop on reply)
- **Day 0:** first-touch DM above (personalized first line + offer the video).
- **Day 3:** "Did you get a chance to watch? Happy to answer anything." + one
  concrete proof point (e.g. "Off-plan was 67% of Q1 2026 Dubai sales").
- **Day 7:** soft close: "I'll leave it here for now — if Dubai's ever on your
  radar, my door's open." Then stop.

## ✅ Rules that keep you credible (and unbanned)
- Personalize every first line; **no identical mass DMs** (LinkedIn flags it).
- Keep daily volume modest (LinkedIn ~20–30 personalized/day max).
- **Every claim true:** training for juniors, referral terms, and the "trip on
  us" is tied to **closing a deal** — say so. No income guarantees.
- Honor "not interested" instantly. Track replies in your Asana (Closer) board.
