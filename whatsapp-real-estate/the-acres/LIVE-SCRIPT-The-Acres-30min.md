# The Acres by Meraas — 30-Minute Live Script
**Host: Marwan Wareth, Property Consultant · Bridges and Allies**
*Glance-sheet for going live. Bold lines = say close to verbatim (hooks, numbers, CTAs). Bullets = riff in your own words. Times are guides, not rules.*

---

## BEFORE YOU GO LIVE (2-min setup)
- Pin a comment: **"Comment ACRES and I'll DM you the full owner's report 📩"**
- Have ready to read out: the 3 headline numbers → **AED 2.4B**, **25 sales**, **AED 1,707/sq ft**.
- Good light, phone landscape or vertical, quiet room, water nearby.
- Title the live: **"The Acres by Meraas — what owners & buyers need to know (live Q&A)"**
- Keep the owner's report PDF open in another tab so you can reference exact cluster prices.

**Golden rule for the whole 30 min:** every 3–4 minutes, do ONE of these — read a new comment out loud, ask a question, or repeat the "comment ACRES" CTA. A live with no interaction dies. Keep it a conversation.

*People are still joining. Don't dump value yet — hook, then stall warmly while numbers build.*

**Verbatim open:**

If you own there, or you've been watching it — you're going to want to stick around for the next 30 minutes, because I'm going to break down exactly what's happening with prices, handover, and whether it's a moment to sell or to hold."

- Introduce yourself: **"I'm Marwan Wareth, property consultant with Bridges and Allies. I pulled the official Dubai Land Department numbers on The Acres, and I'm going to walk you through all of it — no hype, just the data."**
- Stall for joiners: *"Drop in the comments where you're watching from… and if you own at The Acres, tell me your cluster — A, B, C, let's see who's here."*
- Set expectations: *"I'll cover what it is, the real prices, handover timing, and I'll take your questions live at the end. Comment your questions any time and I'll get to them."*

## SEGMENT 2 — WHAT THE ACRES ACTUALLY IS (3:00–7:00)

- **"The Acres is Meraas's first standalone-villa community — and I mean standalone. No townhouses, no apartments, no shared walls. About 1,200 villas in Dubailand, off Emirates Road."**
- The vision — talk to the lifestyle:
  - Built around **swimmable lagoons** — actual sandy-beach lagoons you can swim in, not just water features.
  - The **Halo Loop Park** — a green spine connecting the whole community; every villa within a short walk of a park.
  - Dry **wadi trails**, gardens, and a **retail promenade, school, nursery, clinic and mosque all planned inside** the community.
  - **"This is Meraas — the developer behind City Walk, Bluewaters, La Mer. Their brand is design and lifestyle, and that's what you're buying here: nature in the middle of the city."**
- Engagement beat: *"If you've seen the lagoon renders, drop a 🌿 in the comments — they're honestly some of the best in Dubai right now."*

## SEGMENT 3 — THE VILLAS: WHAT YOU ACTUALLY GET (7:00–12:00)
*Read the sizes; people always ask about size.*

- **3-bedroom:** around **3,048 sq ft** built-up — and note, that's the size of a 4-bed in most other communities. Every villa has a private pool, garden, maid's room, two parking.
- **4-bedroom:** roughly **3,990 to 4,875 sq ft**.
- **5-bedroom:** roughly **4,960 to 6,000 sq ft**.
- **The Acres Estates** — the premium tier: **5, 6 and 7-bedroom mansions, from about 6,900 up to 11,000 sq ft**, in two styles, Amber (contemporary) and Ivory (classic).
- **"So the whole range is family villas at the core, and true mansions at the top."**
- Payment plan: **"Easy entry — 65/35 with just 10% down. You pay 65% during construction, and the final 35% only at handover."**
- Engagement beat: *"Which one's your favourite — 3-bed, 4-bed, or are we going straight for the Estates mansion? Comment below."*

## SEGMENT 4 — THE REAL PRICES (12:00–18:00) ⭐ HEADLINE SEGMENT
*This is why people stay. Be precise. This is DLD data, not asking prices — say that.*

**Verbatim setup:**
> "Now the part everyone actually wants — the numbers. And I want to be clear: these are **real transfers recorded with the Dubai Land Department** over the last five months. Not asking prices. What buyers actually paid."

- **In the last 5 months, 25 villas sold. Average price: 7.88 million dirhams. Average: 1,707 dirhams per square foot.**
- By type (real sold ranges):
  - **3-bed: 5.0 to 5.6 million.**
  - **4-bed: 6.5 to 7.75 million.**
  - **5-bed: 7.0 all the way to 13.5 million** depending on plot and lagoon position.
- The launch comparison — be honest, it builds trust:
  - **"At launch in 2024, 3-beds started at 5.09 million. Today they're trading 5.0 to 5.6. So early buyers are up modestly — this hasn't been a 30% flip, and anyone telling you that isn't showing you the DLD data."**
- **The key insight for buyers:** **"Asking prices on the portals are running about 14% above what actually transfers. That gap is exactly why you negotiate — and why you work with someone who reads the real comps."**
- **The key insight for owners:** **"If your cluster has recent sales, you have fresh, bankable comparables. I can tell you in two minutes what yours is worth today."**
- Engagement beat: *"Owners watching — comment your cluster and I'll tell you if there's been a recent sale near you."*
- CTA: **"And if you want the full breakdown — every cluster, every price — comment ACRES and I'll send you the report."**

## SEGMENT 5 — CONSTRUCTION & HANDOVER, THE HONEST TIMELINE (18:00–22:00)
*Correct the #1 misconception. This honesty is your credibility moment.*

- **"Let's clear something up, because I hear it a lot: nothing at The Acres has handed over yet. It's still under construction."**
- The status: **"Early 2026, the community was around 10% built — foundations done on 220-plus villas, structures going up now."**
- The good news (this is the AED 2.4B hook, paid off): **"Here's why I opened with 2.4 billion — in April, Meraas awarded 2.4 billion dirhams in new construction contracts. Add the 1.9 billion from late last year, and that's over 4 billion dirhams committed. This project is funded and moving."**
- The realistic date: **"First handovers are expected late 2027 into 2028, with the main wave through 2028. So if you buy today, you're roughly 18 months to two years from keys."**
- Why it matters: **"That window — now until handover — is historically when the resale market is thinnest and buyers pay for certainty. It's a real consideration whether you're buying or selling."**

## SEGMENT 6 — SELL, HOLD, OR BUY? + MARKET CONTEXT (22:00–27:00) ⭐ VALUE SEGMENT

**Frame it as advice, not a pitch:**
> "So what do you actually do with this? Let me give you the honest version for each of you watching."

- **If you're an OWNER thinking of selling:**
  - Real demand exists — 25 owners exited in 5 months. **But there are 350-plus listings competing.**
  - **"Priced right, against the DLD evidence, villas sell. Priced on hope, they sit. Lagoon-facing and corner plots are clearing at the top — up to 1,959 a foot."**
- **If you're an OWNER leaning to HOLD:**
  - **"Also smart — and I'll tell you when it's the better move. Communities like Arabian Ranches 3 and Tilal Al Ghaf saw their biggest jumps *after* handover, once homes were real and mortgageable. On the 65/35 plan, your big payment isn't due till keys. The smart play is just knowing your number so you can move when it suits you."**
- **If you're a BUYER:**
  - **"You're buying the best masterplan in the corridor before it exists, at pricing that's basically flat since launch, from a tier-one developer. Negotiate hard — motivated sellers exist. Best value is in the 3 and 4-beds."**
- Market context (keep it brief, sound informed):
  - **"Zoom out: Dubai's market cooled a bit through 2026 after a huge run. But villas are holding up far better than apartments — villa supply is scarce. The watch-item for The Acres specifically is the wave of new villa projects handing over nearby in 2028–29 — Grand Polo, Haven, Athlon. That's the real thing to price in."**
- **"Bottom line: great asset, tier-one developer, fair pricing — but timing and negotiation matter. That's the whole game right now."**
