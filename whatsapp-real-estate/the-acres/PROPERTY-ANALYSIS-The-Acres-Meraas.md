## Executive Summary

The Acres is Meraas's (Dubai Holding) all-standalone-villa master community in Dubailand (Wadi Al Safa 7 per Propsearch): ~1,200 villas of 3–7BR around swimmable lagoons, the Halo Loop Park, wadi trails, an in-community school, clinic and retail promenade. It launched January 2024 at full market pricing (3BR from AED 5.09M ≈ AED 1,670/sq ft) and has since traded essentially sideways: the PM index sits at AED 1,639/sq ft (+1.84% YoY, −2.62% QoQ), while actual transactions average AED 1,707/sq ft.

**Nothing has been handed over.** Construction was ~9–12% complete as of Feb–Mar 2026 (RERA/developer inspections); first handovers are guided Q4 2027–Q1 2028, with staged completion running to roughly August 2028 and Estates Phase 3 at Q3 2028. Meraas awarded AED 2.4bn in new construction contracts in April 2026 — delivery conviction is real, but buyers today are buying a masterplan, not a community.

The honest picture: a top-tier product (the only standalone-only lagoon community in the corridor) at fair-to-full pricing, in a market that has cooled from its Q1 2026 peak, with ~350 resale listings competing against continuing developer releases and a heavy 2027–2029 corridor supply wave (Grand Polo Club's 6,661 units land right after The Acres' handover). Best suited to end-users and patient holders targeting the post-handover re-rating (realistically +10–20% in 2028–29); weak for flippers — some Phase 1 resales are already listed below original price.
