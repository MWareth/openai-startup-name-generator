The decision

Sell now, or hold to handover? An honest view

If you're considering selling

There is real demand — 25 owners exited in five months. But with 350+ competing listings, three things decide whether you transact:

  • Evidence-based pricing. Deals close at AED 1,640–1,710/sq ft. We price from your cluster's DLD comparables, not hope.
  • Plot story. Lagoon-facing, corner and large-plot villas are clearing at the top of the range — up to AED 1,959/sq ft.
  • Buyer reach. The active buyers now are end-users planning their 2028 move — they buy from brokers who can show them the data.

If you're leaning towards holding

Also a strong position — and we'll tell you when it's the better one:

  • Communities like Arabian Ranches 3 and Tilal Al Ghaf saw their strongest price moves after handover, once homes became real and mortgageable.
  • On the 65/35 plan, your final 35% isn't due until handover — time is on your side.
  • The smart move today is simply knowing your number — so you can act the moment the market or your plans change.
