The project

Villas around lagoons, built by Dubai Holding's Meraas

The Acres is a master community of about 1,200 villas in Dubailand, next to Emirates Road. Every home is a standalone villa with its own garden, pool and parking — there are no shared walls anywhere in the community.

The masterplan puts nature at the centre: swimmable lagoons with a lagoon clubhouse, a looping central park connecting every neighbourhood, and dry wadi walking trails. A school, nursery, clinic, mosque and a retail promenade are all planned inside the community, and every villa sits within a short walk of a park.

Construction & handover

Nothing is handed over yet — here's the honest timeline

Price trend

Prices climbed through 2025, then cooled — good news if you're buying

The Acres — average price per sq ft (AED)

Property Monitor Dynamic Price Index, June 2025 – June 2026

Prices rose steadily through 2025, peaked around AED 1,683 per sq ft, and have eased about 2.6% over the last quarter to AED 1,639 — still up on last year. The whole Dubai market took a breather in spring 2026, and here it means one thing for buyers: sellers are negotiable, and deals are closing at or below the numbers in the table above. Windows like this rarely stay open once a community starts handing over.

Our take

Three things to know before you offer

Negotiate — the data is on your side. Advertised prices average well above what actually transfers. Fair value today is AED 1,640–1,710 per sq ft. We anchor every offer to recorded sales, not listings.
This is a 2028 move-in, and a hold — not a quick flip. You're buying the corridor's best masterplan before it exists. Communities like this historically re-rate after handover, once the lagoons fill and families move in. Buy it to live in or to hold through 2028–29.
3 and 4 bedroom villas are the sweet spot. They have the most sales evidence, the strongest future rental demand (est. AED 280–370K/year at handover), and the deepest resale market. The 5BR+ range is beautiful but thinner — plot and lagoon position matter enormously there.
