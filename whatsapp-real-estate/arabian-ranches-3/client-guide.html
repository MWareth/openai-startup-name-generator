Community

An established, fully alive community

Arabian Ranches 3 is an Emaar community that's largely handed over and lived-in — families moved in, schools and retail open, and Ghaf Woods Mall right on the doorstep just 0.6 km away.

That maturity is exactly why it's so liquid: 25 villas transferred in about three weeks, from AED 2.3M in Joy up to AED 7.25M in Elie Saab. For owners that means a real, working resale market — buyers can mortgage, view a finished home, and move straight in.

The decision

Sell now, or hold? An honest view

If you're considering selling

  • Price to your cluster. 3-beds in Anya, Raya and Spring are clearing AED 2.75–3.05M; larger 4-beds in Caya and Elie Saab reach AED 6.5–7.25M. We price from your exact sub-community, not a community average.
  • Liquidity is your friend. With this many buyers active, a well-priced villa moves quickly. Presentation and the right asking number decide who transacts.
  • Bank valuations are supportive. RICS valuations averaging ~AED 1,687/sq ft help buyers finance, which widens your buyer pool.

If you're leaning to hold

  • Arabian Ranches 3 has a deep, established rental market — a handed-over Emaar community with schools and amenities is always in family demand.
  • A dedicated Dubai Metro link is planned for the community, a long-term value driver.
  • The smart move today is simply knowing your number, so you can act the moment it suits you.
