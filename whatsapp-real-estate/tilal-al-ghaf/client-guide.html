Price trend

Up on last year, cooler than the spring peak

Property Monitor Dynamic Price Index

Dubai villa market trend · June 2025 – June 2026

The wider Dubai villa index cooled about 2.6% this quarter after a strong 2025. Tilal Al Ghaf itself is transacting at an average of AED 2,032 per sq ft — well above the market — with its benchmark Harmony villas clearing AED 2,300–3,110 per sq ft.

Community

Dubai's benchmark lagoon community, delivered

Tilal Al Ghaf is Majid Al Futtaim's flagship community, built around a swimmable lagoon with 400 metres of beachfront and 70,000 sq m of water — and it's largely delivered and lived-in.

That makes it one of Dubai's most active villa resale markets: 25 sales in about three weeks, from AED 3.5M townhouses in Elan and Aura Gardens to an AED 22M Elysian mansion. Harmony is the benchmark address, and it commands the premium.

The decision

Sell now, or hold? An honest view

If you're considering selling

  • Lifestyle commands a premium. Harmony villas are clearing AED 2,300–3,110/sq ft; Amara and Alaya sit a little below. We price to your exact sub-community and plot.
  • Every size is liquid. From AED 3.5M townhouses to AED 22M mansions, buyers are active across the whole range — rare depth for sellers.
  • The lagoon is scarce. Few communities offer a true swimmable-beach lifestyle, and that scarcity supports pricing.

If you're leaning to hold

  • Tilal Al Ghaf has already proven the post-handover re-rating — early Harmony buyers are well in profit, and the lagoon lifestyle keeps demand deep.
  • Rental demand for lagoon-facing and larger villas is among the strongest in the city.
  • The smart move today is simply knowing your number, so you can act the moment it suits you.
