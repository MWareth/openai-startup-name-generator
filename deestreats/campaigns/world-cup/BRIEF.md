# World Cup Campaign — Brief

Six menu items, one cinematic language. Each item is a 3-shot story that ends on a Send Message CTA. Built for Meta, Dubai-targeted, mute-first.

## Three-shot structure (every item)
1. **Stadium** — establish the arena energy. Item enters the "pitch."
2. **Trophy / Walkout** — the hero beat. Item presented like a trophy lift or player walkout.
3. **Showcase** — close-up payoff. Texture, the slice/spoon, the order CTA.

## Mandatory on every cut
- **First 1–2s:** brand-brown + cream frame, logo + item name visible.
- **Every shot:** super in NF One Little Font Bold (ad) / Lemonade (video).
- **End card:** `DM to order 🏆 @deestreats.ae` + Send Message prompt.
- **Style:** medium-low motion (Kling primary), cinematic, warm. Cohesive across all six.

## Legal — generic kit only
No real footballers, no named athletes, no real club crests. Describe kits generically:
- "home kit in brand-brown & cream", "keeper gloves", "captain's armband", "boots laced up", "walkout tunnel lights". Never tie to a real person or team.
